HyperOSUnf*cker helps tune HyperOS devices with Shizuku-backed local ADB shell commands.

Features:
- HyperOS performance, memory, display, standby, and battery tuning
- Named HyperOS presets
- Debloat tools for installed and removed apps
- Logs with level filtering
- Experimental visual flags hidden behind a warning section

Requirements:
- Shizuku must be installed, running, and authorized
- USB debugging is recommended for recovery and troubleshooting

System tuning and debloat actions can affect device stability. Review each option before applying changes.
